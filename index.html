Dear Paapuuu,

I know I hurt you, Paapuuu, and I am deeply sorry. You deserved patience, care, and understanding from me, and I failed to give that to you in that moment.

I am not writing this to force forgiveness. I am writing because what we have means too much to leave behind careless words and silent regret.

If your heart allows it, I would love the chance to make things right, not only by saying sorry, but by being better where it truly counts.

What I miss most

Your voice, your little reactions, our inside jokes, and the peace I felt just knowing you were there. That space still feels like yours.

What I hope for

One more conversation. One honest chance. One moment where I can show you that my apology is more than beautiful words on a page.

My Promise

If you let me, I will do better.

Promises matter only when they become actions, so keep these real if you edit them.

Listen better

I will hear your feelings before reacting with my ego.

Real love needs patience. I want to respond with care, not defensiveness.

Communicate clearly

I will say what I feel honestly, before silence turns into distance.

I do not want confusion to stand where clarity and tenderness should be.

Respect your heart

I will never treat your love like something guaranteed.

You deserve consistency, reassurance, and the kind of effort that feels safe.

Prove it quietly

I know growth is shown over time, not in one emotional message.

If I get another chance, I want my actions to speak more than this page does.
